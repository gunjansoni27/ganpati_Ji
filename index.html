About Us

Ganpati Compressor and
Tractor Work

GCTW, short for Ganpati Compressor and Tractor Works, traces its roots back to the year 2000 when it was established by Sh. Ashok Kumar, the current Chairman of the company. Sh. Ashok Kumar's remarkable journey is one of sheer determination and resilience, having commenced his entrepreneurial voyage at a tender age. Hailing from the picturesque Gurdaspur district of Punjab, Sh. Ashok Kumar embarked on a life-changing journey that eventually led him to the bustling city of Faridabad. At the age of 17, he, along with his elder brother, set out on a quest for a better life and greater opportunities.
